A Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."

Early Life

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Coming from a humble background, he worked hard to support his education and completed his studies in physics and aerospace engineering.

Contributions

Known as the "Missile Man of India", Dr. Kalam played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007 and was a beloved leader known for his simplicity and integrity.
